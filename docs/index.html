  • Key projects (Detail information is in the Notion portfolio)
    • Development of Face Restoration Model for YouTube thumbnail image
      • Collaborated with KBS Media Lab for research and development.
      • Fancam videos of individuals from the music broadcast program such as Music Bank are uploaded to YouTube. The quality of these videos' thumbnail images often deteriorates due to various reasons such as movements in dancing, camera shaking, and lighting conditions.
      • Existing SOTA models of Facial Restoration often alter the individual's identity features, especially causing distortions in the shape of the eyes.
      • Therefore, I developed the custom model that enhances the quality of thumbnail images while preserving identity features of the individuals.
    • Development of Korean Face Image Generation Model
      • The public dataset named FFHQ by Nvidia, commonly used for training generative models like StyleGAN, enables the generation of high-quality human face images. However, it has the limitation of primarily generating images of Western faces.
      • To address this, I built the Korean face dataset and conducted training to develop the generative model specifically for Korean face images.
      • In addition to simply creating a face image, I implemented a function that can control features such as age and gender by exploring latent space.
    • Virtual Hair Dyeing
      • Accurately distinguishing the hair area in an image at pixel level and naturally transforming the hair color to a user's desired color is a challenging task.
      • I developed a model for virtual hair dyeing that outperforms several commercial applications(such as Naver Snow, KakaoHairShop, and L'Oréal) in terms of quality.
